Three Involutions

Complement · Reciprocal · Third — acting simultaneously

Working note · K. Forseth · May 2026

An interactive diagram for Mats

Three order-2 transformations of the anharmonic group acting simultaneously on the projective line. Drag along the horizontal axis to move x.

Complement x→1−x: red arcs above the axis. Swaps 0↔1, fixes ∞. One axis, compass construction only.

Reciprocal x→1/x: blue arcs below the axis. Swaps 0↔∞, fixes 1. One axis, straightedge construction only.

Third involution x→x/(x−1): green line crossing to the vertical axis. Fixes 0 and 2. Requires both axes — its hinge (1,1) is forced by the unit hyperbola xy=1 meeting the diagonal x=y.
